STMicroelectronics Mailbox Test Driver

Required properties:
- compatible		: "st,mailbox-test"
- mboxes		: phandles to sta-mailbox DT node(s)
			  The (n*2) cells are sta-mailbox contoller phandles
			  The ((n+1)*2) cells give the channel number dedicated
			  to the test driver.

See sta-mailbox.txt for the mailbox controller bindings.

Examples:

	mailbox_test {
		compatible = "st,mailbox-test";
		mboxes = <&mailbox 1 &mailbox 2 &mailbox 3
			  &mailbox 4 &mailbox 5 &mailbox 6
			  &mailbox 7 &mailbox 8 &mailbox 9
			  &mailbox 10 &mailbox 11 &mailbox 12
			  &mailbox 13 &mailbox 14 &mailbox 15>;
	};
